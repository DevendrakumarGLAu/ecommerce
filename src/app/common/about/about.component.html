About Firozabad Bangles

Welcome to Firozabad Bangles, your premier destination for authentic handmade glass bangles. Located in Firozabad, the city famous for its centuries-old tradition of bangle making, we bring the beauty of traditional craftsmanship directly to your doorstep.

Each bangle is carefully handcrafted by skilled artisans, ensuring unmatched quality and vibrant designs that celebrate Indian culture and heritage. Whether it's festive collections or everyday wear, our bangles are designed to add elegance and charm to every outfit.

Our mission is simple: to preserve the art of Firozabad bangle-making and make it accessible to the world. Every purchase supports local artisans and keeps this historic craft alive.

  • Authentic Firozabad glass and designs.
  • Wide range of colors, patterns, and sizes.
  • Perfect for festivals, weddings, and gifting.
  • Supporting traditional craftsmanship.
